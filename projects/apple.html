Engineering Program Manager


Improving speech recognition and understanding for Siri




Overview



My goal is to impact and empower people through technology; this includes exploring how personal agents can better understand speech and intent.

I am interested in broad questions that relate to designing for better social interaction, including: how can we design for more natural interactions and create more conversational experiences?; how can hardware design improve detection of speech?; how might we anticipate context and other factors (e.g. intonation) to improve interpretation?; can speech signals provide useful data about physical and mental health?

As an Engineering Program Manager (EPM) Intern of the Siri EPM team, I contributed to helping Siri. Broadly, this touched on understanding user requests and exploring advancements to make Siri more helpful and conversational.
